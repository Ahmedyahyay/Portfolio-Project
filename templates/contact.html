Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this service?

Our nutrition assistance is designed specifically for adults with BMI ≥ 30, following evidence-based health-centric patterns for obesity management.

How is BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated as weight (kg) divided by height squared (m²). We use height in centimeters following copilot health-centric patterns.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade security with password hashing and input validation following strict privacy guidelines.

How are nutrition scores calculated?

Our nutrition scoring system prioritizes high protein (≥15g), good fiber (≥3g), and appropriate calorie counts (≤400) for the BMI ≥ 30 demographic.
